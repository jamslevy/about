About Me

From machine learning and biohacking to AR/VR and blockchain, I have worked with frontier technologies for over a decade and am continuing to hack future tech.

I live with my wife and cat in Oakland California.

My Interests

Decentralization

Just as the distributed networking of information has led to world-changing shifts in search, social networking, and publishing, we are now seeing a shift toward the distributed networking of economic value facilitating many types of trust-minimized systems.

Spatial Computing

While media has become increasingly smaller and more handheld, over the next decade we will see a shift toward media content that is displayed on the smallest screens yet - mounted in front of our eyes - and is able to fill and augment the world around us.

Mobile & Web Software

Whether it is for an iOS or Android app used by millions or just for a weekend hackathon, I most prefer to work with React, React Native, Node and Python but enjoy working with Rust, Solidity, Raspberry Pi, and many other types of technologies.

My Career Journey

2007-2010 Investigative Reporting

Having earned a bachelor's degree in journalism in 2008 from Northwestern University, my career started as the online editor for the Daily Northwestern and an internship developing online projects at San Francisco Magazine. I quickly learned that working with an expertise with querying, processing and presenting data was crucial to storytelling in the current age of networked information.

2011-2013 Built and Sold a Startup

While working on an ebook about HTML5, I learned that ebook analytics were far behind their web and mobile counterparts, and decided to address the lack of metrics for authors and founded Hiptype to develop an analytics plugin for Kindle and iBooks. It was backed by Y-Combinator and acquired by Urban Airship in 2013.

2013-2016 Web, iOS, and Android Apps at SurveyMonkey

I joined the engineering team at SurveyMonkey, where I learned about delivering production-grade code to millions throughout the world using a high-scalability architecture. Among other contributions, I developed a new React-based app allowing surveys to be created, shared and analyzed on mobile web, iOS and Android.

2016-2019 Profitable VR Startup

When I first tried the Oculus Rift developer kit, it was clear that I was witnessing the very beginning of a new paradigm of immersive technology. I built WeLens, a profitable VR software startup, as founder and CEO. WeLens has worked with almost all major film festivals, dozens of top marketers and retail brands, numerous artists and filmmakers, and both Eminem and Sir Elton John.

2018-2019 Decentralized Finance

As an early contributor to Ethereum, I have been involved with cryptocurrency and blockchain projects since 2014. I am currently working on packaging the various benefits of trustless decentralized finance systems replacing outdated Wall Street infrastructure for mainstream users, with a focus on simplicity and usability.
