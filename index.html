My Career Journey

2011-2013 Mobile Analytics

While working on an ebook about HTML5, I learned that ebook analytics were far behind their web and mobile counterparts, and decided to address the lack of metrics for authors and founded Hiptype to develop an analytics plugin for Kindle and iBooks. It was backed by Y-Combinator and acquired by Urban Airship in 2013.

2013-2016 Web, iOS, and Android Apps at SurveyMonkey

I joined the engineering team at SurveyMonkey, where I learned about delivering production-grade code to millions throughout the world using a high-scalability architecture. Among other contributions, I developed a new React-based app allowing surveys to be created, shared and analyzed on mobile web, iOS and Android.

2016-2019 Enterprise VR Software

When I first tried the Oculus Rift developer kit, it was clear that I was witnessing the very beginning of a new paradigm of immersive technology. I built WeLens, a profitable VR software startup, as founder and CEO. WeLens has worked with almost all major film festivals, dozens of top marketers and retail brands, numerous artists and filmmakers, and both Eminem and Sir Elton John.

2019 Decentralized Finance & ML

As an early contributor to Ethereum, I have been interested in decentralization technology since 2014. I developed a trustless decentralized financial robo-advisor app, and EnclaveML, a framework for privacy-preserving machine learning.

2019- Self-Driving Vehicle Tools

I am currently building fleet automation software at Cruise for managing AV software releases, facilitating research and development, and remotely supporting fleet operations.
